Your portal

All your ContabIA clients. One view.

Portfolio dashboard: See all your ContabIA clients in one place. Close status, open exceptions, pending reviews — at a glance.

Exception queue: Items that need your professional judgment — prioritized by criticality, with full documentation and agent recommendations.

Agent conversation: Discuss exceptions with the agent by chat. Ask "why did you categorize this as utilities?" and get the reasoning with source documents.
