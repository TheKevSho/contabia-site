Talk to your agent

How it works

Connect. Talk. Receive.

Give us your credentials. The agent does the rest — connects, maps your chart of accounts, detects your channels, imports your employees, and configures everything. You confirm, not configure.

1

Connect

Give us your credentials for Alegra, Siigo, or WorldOffice. Connect your LobbyPMS or Cloudbeds. The agent pulls your chart of accounts, maps it to reporting standards, detects your OTA channels and commission rates, imports your employee roster, and identifies your tax configuration — NIT, municipality, CIIU code, ICA rate, autorretenedor status.

You confirm what the agent found. You don't fill in forms.

2

Talk

Send receipts by WhatsApp as you get them — photos or PDFs. Ask questions about your numbers anytime. Enable daily flash reports if you want morning updates. The agent works 24/7, ingesting transactions, reconciling data sources, and monitoring for anomalies between closes.

The agent is a team member, not a tool. You interact with it like you would a senior bookkeeper who never sleeps.

3

Receive

Every month, the agent closes your books — reconciliation, tax calculations, employee liabilities, journal entries, all in parallel. Your accountant receives the review package, approves or modifies entries, and signs off. You receive your complete monthly workbook via WhatsApp and email. The dashboard stays current between closes.

Nothing posts to your accounting system without your accountant's approval. The agent proposes, the human decides.
