Your workflow with ContabIA

From receipts to reports. You talk, the agent works.

Day to day

Send receipts. Ask questions.

Send receipts by WhatsApp as you get them. The agent extracts, categorizes, and stages them instantly. Ask anything about your numbers — "how are sales this week?" "what did we spend on utilities?" — and get answers from your real data.

Biweekly

Cash flow forecast updated.

Every quincena, your agent updates your cash flow forecast. See what's coming — payroll, prima payments, tax deadlines, vendor obligations — before it hits your bank account.

Daily (optional)

Flash report — revenue, occupancy, cash.

Enable daily flash reports via WhatsApp. Every morning: yesterday's revenue by channel, occupancy, cash position. Disable anytime by telling the agent.

Month-end

The agent closes your books.

The agent pulls final data from PMS, banks, and OTAs. Runs reconciliation, tax calculations, employee liabilities — all in parallel. Generates proposed entries and an exception queue. Packages everything for your accountant to review. Hours, not days.

Delivery

Your complete monthly package.

P&L (departmental for hospitality), balance sheet, cash flow summary, bank reconciliation, tax summary, KPI dashboard, receipt deductibility report, and cash flow forecast. Delivered via WhatsApp and email in xlsx, pdf, and pptx.

Tax recovery

Your foreign purchases probably aren't deductible right now.

Every payment to a foreign vendor — software subscriptions, cloud hosting, digital ads, OTA commissions, e-commerce — requires a documento soporte to be tax-deductible in Colombia. Without it, you pay the expense AND lose the deduction. Your agent generates them automatically for every foreign purchase.
